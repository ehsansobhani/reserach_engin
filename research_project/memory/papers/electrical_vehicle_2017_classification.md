## Abstract

 The rapid emergence of electric vehicles (EVs) demands an advanced infrastructure of publicly accessible charging stations that provide efficient charging services. In this paper, we propose a new charging station operation mechanism, the JoAP, which jointly optimizes the EV admission control, pricing, and charging scheduling to maximize the charging station's profit.
