## Abstract

 Location of Base Stations (BS) in mobile networks plays an important role in coverage and received signal strength. As Internet ofThings (IoT), autonomous vehicles and smart cities evolve, wireless net-work coverage will have an important role in ensuring seamless connectivity. Due to use of higher carrier frequencies, blockages cause communication to primarily be Line of Sight (LoS), increasing the importance of base station placement.
