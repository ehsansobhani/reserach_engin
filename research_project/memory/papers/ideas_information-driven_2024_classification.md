## Abstract

 Our work delves into user behaviour at Electric Vehicle(EV) charging stations during peak times, particularly focusing on how impatience drives balking (not joining queues) and reneging (leaving queues prematurely). We introduce an Agent-based simulation framework that incorporates user optimism levels (pessimistic, standard, and optimistic) in the queue dynamics.
