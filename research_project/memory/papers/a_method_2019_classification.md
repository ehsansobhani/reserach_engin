## Abstract

 Electric vehicles (EVs) are increasingly used in transportation. Worldwide use of EVs, for their limited battery capacity, calls for effective planning of EVs charging stations to enhance the efficiency of using EVs. This paper provides a methodology of describing EV detouring behavior for recharging, and based on this, we adopt the extra driving length caused by detouring and the length of uncompleted route as the indicators of evaluating an EV charging station deployment plan.
