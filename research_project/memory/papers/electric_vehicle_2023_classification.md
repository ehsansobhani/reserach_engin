## Abstract

 We study electric vehicle (EV) fleet and charging infrastructure planning in a spatial setting. With customer requests arriving continuously at rate $λ$ throughout the day, we determine the minimum number of vehicles and chargers for a target service level, along with matching and charging policies. While non-EV systems require extra $Θ(λ^{2/3})$ vehicles due to pickup times, EV systems differ.
