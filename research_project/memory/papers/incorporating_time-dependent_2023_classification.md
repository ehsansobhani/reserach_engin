## Abstract

 A massive use of electric vehicles is nowadays considered to be a key element of a sustainable transportation policy and the availability of charging stations is a crucial issue for their extensive use. Charging stations in an urban area have to be deployed in such a way that they can satisfy a demand that may dramatically vary in space and time.
