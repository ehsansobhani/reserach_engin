## Abstract

 Electrification of marine transport is a promising solution to reduce sector greenhouse gas emissions and operational costs. However, the large upfront cost of electric vessels and the required charging infrastructure can be a barrier to the development of this technology. Optimization algorithms that jointly design the charging infrastructure and the operation of electric vessels can help to reduce these costs and make these projects viable.
