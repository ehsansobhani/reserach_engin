## Abstract

 Vehicular control systems are required to be both extremely reliable and robust to different environmental conditions, e.g. load or tire-road friction. In this paper, we extend a new paradigm for state estimation, called Twin-in-the-Loop filtering (TiL-F), to the estimation of the unknown parameters describing the vehicle operating conditions.
