## Abstract

 Increasing the adoption of Electric Vehicles (EV) is an integral part of many strategies to address climate change and air pollution. However, Electric Vehicle adoption rates are inhibited by several factors which reduce the confidence of potential buyers of electric vehicles, namely range anxiety and limited charging infrastructure.
