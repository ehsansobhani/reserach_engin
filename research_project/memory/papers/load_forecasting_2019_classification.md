## Abstract

 Charging demands of electric vehicles (EVs) are sharply increasing due to the rapid development of EVs. Hence, reliable and convenient quick charge stations are required to respond to the needs of EV drivers. Due to the uncertainty of EV charging loads, load forecasting becomes vital for the operation of quick charge stations to formulate the day-ahead plan.
