## Abstract

 This article presents the problem of locating electric vehicle (EV) charging stations in a city by defining the Electric Vehicle Charging Stations Locations (EV-CSL) problem. The idea is to minimize the distance the citizens have to travel to charge their vehicles. EV-CSL takes into account the maximum number of charging stations to install and the electric power requirements.
