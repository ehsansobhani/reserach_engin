## Abstract

 In this paper, we propose an algorithm that extracts spatial frequent patterns to explain the relative characteristics of a specific location from the available social data. This paper proposes a spatial social data model which includes spatial social data, spatial support, spatial frequent patterns, spatial partition, and spatial clustering; these concepts are used for describing the exploration algorithm of spatial frequent patterns.
