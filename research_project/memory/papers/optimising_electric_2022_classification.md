## Abstract

 We present a new model for finding the optimal placement of electric vehicle charging stations across a multi-period time frame so as to maximise electric vehicle adoption. Via the use of advanced discrete choice models and user classes, this work allows for a granular modelling of user attributes and their preferences in regard to charging station characteristics.
