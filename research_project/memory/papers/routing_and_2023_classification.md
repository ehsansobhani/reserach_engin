## Abstract

 This paper studies the routing and charging behaviors of electric vehicles in a competitive ride-hailing market. When the vehicles are idle, they can choose whether to continue cruising to search for passengers, or move a charging station to recharge. The behaviors of individual vehicles are then modeled by a Markov decision process (MDP).
